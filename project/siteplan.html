Overview

This document outlines the site plan for Hair By Pretty Rukky, an online platform for selling wigs and hairstyling services.

Site Name

Hair By Pretty Rukky - This name reflects the elegance and luxury of our wigs and hairstyling services.

Site Purpose

The website will showcase high-quality wigs, hairstyling services, customer testimonials, and booking options.

Scenarios

  • Where can I view and purchase high-quality wigs?
  • How do I book a hairstyling appointment?

Color Schema

Primary Color: Deep Purple (#800080) - Used for headings, navigation, and accents.

Accent Color: Gold (#FFD700) - Used for buttons and call-to-action elements.

Background: White (#FFFFFF) - Ensures a clean and elegant look.

Typography

  • Playfair Display - Used for headings and brand name.
  • Poppins - Used for body text.
  • Raleway - Used for buttons and call-to-action text.
