Site Name

GlamHair Studio - This name represents a stylish and modern salon that specializes in wigs and hairstyling services.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an online platform for showcasing hairstyling services, selling high-quality wigs, and allowing customers to book appointments easily.

Scenarios

  • Where can I browse available wig styles and purchase online?
  • How do I book an appointment for hairstyling services?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: Blush Pink (#F4C2C2) - Used for headings, buttons, and accents.

Secondary Color: Soft Gray (#7D7D7D) - Used for text and backgrounds.

Typography

Headings: Playfair Display

Body Text: Poppins

Buttons: Raleway
